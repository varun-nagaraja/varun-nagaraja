I currently work on developing on-device AI applications for Facebook Augmented Reality devices.

2016-20: Machine Learning Scientist at Amazon Alexa. I worked on building wakeword detection models for Alexa integrated edge devices with low computational constraints.

2010-16: Ph.D in Computer Science, University of Maryland, College Park. I worked on computer vision problems with my advisor Prof. Larry Davis.

2006-10: B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engg., National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal (India). Back then, I loved building robots.
